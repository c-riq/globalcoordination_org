globalcoordination.org

We aim to enable the enforcement of human rights and international rules through digitial real-time global coordination tools.

Preventing wars, and mitigating abuse of power are essential steps towards a world where human rights are respected. How is it possible that nations still fight wars?
We believe that most people want peace and that wars are fundamentally enabled by the abuse of power and by the lack of effective coordination. In 1946, after having contributed to the >50 million deaths of world war 2, Hermann GÃ¶ring, a leading member of the Nazi Party shared his views on the causes of war:

"Naturally, the common people don't want war; neither in Russia, nor in England, nor for that matter in Germany. That is understood. But, after all, it is the leaders of the country who determine the policy, and it is always a simple matter to drag the people along, whether it is a democracy, or a fascist dictatorship, or a parliament, or a communist dictatorship."

Assuming this to be true, preventing large scale wars requires highly effective coordination among all people.

In order to have peace, there needs to be effective enforcement of international rules.

The global, real time decentralised coordination platform stated.ai can:

  • enable faster and more frequent negotiation among many groups of organisations (government or otherwise)
  • provide increased transparency for public scrutiny on the actions of representatives
  • greatly improve the reaction time, precision and increased participation of collective rule enforcement

To enable coordination, stated.ai establishes online identities through a web of many transparent independent verifications among it's members.

Once established, these high confidence online identities then enable collective contract signatures and polls, which then facilitate fast paced negotiation and enforcement of rules.

How do we get there?

  • Help companies, local governments and non-profit organisations to coordinate more effectively on a global scale using stated.ai. If reliability, resilience, transparency and effectiveness have ensued, then national governments should follow the adoption of stated.ai.
  • Enable individuals to express their preferences in real time using ip-vote.com. Where every IP address can be used to vote. After correcting for manipulation attempts and uneven distribution of IP addresses, this can be a proxy for popular opinion in order to signal the abuse of power.
